Work Experience

Software Engineering Intern at AvTech Corp.

For the past two summers now, I've been an intern for the software engineering department at AvTech Corp., creating and improving programs relating to the aviation industry. My most interesting projects have been a Python application that efficiently and securely communicates with proximity beacons via LoRaWAN technology, and a VBA (Excel) schedule generator to help airport staff assign drivers to service flights throughout the workday. For both projects, I actively communicated with clients and wrote up detailed project reports to guide users through setup and execution of the programs. Additionally, I have spent some time parsing through huge codebases to implement new features and find bugs within existing applications, primarily in Java. I also did a little bit of testing of both hardware and software.

Discrete Structures (CS 173) Course Assistant at UIUC

In Spring of 2023, I was a course assistant for the introductory discrete mathematics class for computer science majors. During this time, I held scheduleded office hours, attended discussion sections, an answered student questions on Discord and course forums to help teach students aboutn fundamental computer science topics. Such topics include algorithm analysis, boolean algebra, recursion, sets, graphs, and trees.
